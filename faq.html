LearnAnintern

  • whatshotWhat does LearnAnintern stand for?
    LearnAnIntern is a proposed initiative undertaken by the students of our esteemed institution K.J. Somaiya Institute of Engineering and Information Technology, where, we want to provide our students with internship opportunities within the boundaries of our college, so that our students can save their time and energy on finding internship opportunities outside the framework of the institution.
  • gradeA word of note
    As per AICTE data , out of 15.87 lakh students at 10,328 technical institutions, only 6.96 lakh students get placements. As we are aware, the prime reason for low employment rate among engineering graduates is lack of basic skills which the industry demands. Engineering colleges face a major challenge when it comes to providing their students live projects and internships. This has come in the wake of declining rate of employment among engineering graduates. This initiative aims to provide a solution to the challenges faced by engineering institutes. There is no better way of learning any engineering concepts until those engineering concepts are practically implemented.
  • call_splitHow are the projects segregated on the website?
    The projects are segregated into two parts. The yet to be started projects viz Abstract and the second part is the archive of the completed projects
